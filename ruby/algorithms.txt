Common task of Wash a Car



- Start off by getting soap, bucket, sponge, & dry towel ready before washing

- Rinse bucket with water to make sure it’s clean before putting soap inside

- Fill the bucket half way then put the soap inside before filling it all the way

- Put the sponge inside the bucket with the soap and water

- Now spray the car with water, making sure you get every inch wet before you start washing

- Start from the front end of the vehicle and work your way to the back

- Clean the wheels & rims

- Dry the car and wheels with a microfiber towel

- Windex everywhere window inside and out

- You’re DONE!
